# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
*   **Clarity and Conciseness:** Emphasize clear, direct communication in all user-facing text and internal documentation. Avoid jargon where possible.

## User Experience
*   **Consistent User Experience:** Ensure a unified look and feel across the client and microfrontend, adhering to modern UI/UX principles.
*   **Performance-Oriented:** Prioritize fast loading times and responsive interactions, especially given the microfrontend architecture.
